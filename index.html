I Am
Sir Tim Berners-Lee

I Am The Founder Of www: World Wide Web

Hello!

In 2004, Berners-Lee was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II for his pioneering work. In 2007, he was elected a member of the National Academy of Engineering for being principally responsible for inventing the World Wide Web. He devised and implemented the first Web browser and Web server and helped foster the Web's subsequent explosive development. He currently directs the W3 Consortium, developing tools and standards to further the Web's potential. In April 2009, he was elected a Foreign Associate of the National Academy of Sciences.
